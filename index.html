About Us

The Problem

Authentic offline connections are declining due to endless scrolling. It’s hard to meet organically, find local friends, or date safely nearby. Existing platforms don’t specialise in hyper-local physical connections.

Our Solution

Jel Me Up is a hyper-local app that lets you meet people within a ~100m radius. We start with Dating, then expand to Networking and Friends—powered by interest tags, in-person meetups and strong safety controls.

Market

India online dating is projected at $350M+ by 2026 (20% CAGR). TAM ≈ 400M urban users (18–35). Hyper-local, offline-first competitors are scarce.

Audience

Ages 18–35 across metro to Tier 2/3 cities; heavy IG/Snap/Tinder users; crave instant & safe offline encounters.

Brand Values

Safety, privacy and consent; real-world serendipity; playful yet respectful energy; bold, vibrant, optimistic tone.

Features

Everything you need to meet right around you.

Real-time Hyper-local Discovery

See people within ~100m with proximity-aware visibility to encourage safe, real meets.

Smart Matchmaking

Match by interests, context, and shared activity; expand later into networking & friend discovery.

Safety & Consent

Verified profiles, user approval and granular visibility controls keep interactions respectful.

Event Mode

Magically useful at live venues, colleges and co-working spaces for instant intros.

Freemium + Boosts

Core features free; premium boosts, virtual gifts, paid icebreakers & venue promos.

Go-to-Market

Campus & co-working clusters, influencer stories, corporate/community tie-ins, viral nearby invites.

Why Jel Me Up?

Competitors are great at scale or community, but few are offline-first with real-time proximity across dating, networking and friends—our all-in-one edge.
